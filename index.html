❤️

Tere haath ka saath na chhodunga kabhi,

Zindagi ki har raah mein, tu hai dua meri.

Kabhi kuch baatein dard de jayein,

Par chahta hoon, tu hamesha muskurayein.

Tere gham mein, teri khushi mein,

Mera saaya ho, tu kabhi na akeli ho.

Main apne aap ko behtar bana raha hoon,

Tere liye, bas tere liye, hamesha tere saath hoon.
